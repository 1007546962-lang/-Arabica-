ABOUT US

%Arabica is a Kyoto-born specialty coffee brand driven by a passion for quality, simplicity, and design. Our philosophy, “See the world through coffee,” inspires every part of our journey—from sourcing beans at origin farms to serving each cup with precision and care.

We work closely with farmers across Latin America, Africa, and Asia to secure beans that express the unique character of their regions. Roasting is performed with meticulous control in small batches to highlight balance, clarity, and sweetness in every cup. Whether espresso or pour-over, each method is treated as craftsmanship—simple, honest, and exact.
